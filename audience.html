Target Audience

The target audience of the site, Rexburg Weather, would be anyone in the Madison County area looking to see what the forecast is for the upcoming days. This includes students, teachers, blue-collar workers, farmers and everyone in between.

Two Personas

Gender: Male
Age: 14-16
Income: $0/year
Occupation: Student
Reason For Checking: Choosing what clothes to wear to school that day, and what would be appropriate when taking the weather into account.

Gender: Female
Age: 36-40
Income: $42k-$44k/year
Occupation: P.E Teacher
Reason For Checking: Deciding on what activites to plan for Middle School Physical Education classes and if outside activities are possible with the weather.

Scenarios

Some scenarios for someone might use this website include...
A student needs to decide whether to wear extra layers,
A farmer needs to check the weather for his crops,
An uber driver needs to see what he should set the resting temperature of his car at for their cliental,
A teacher is planning an outdoor fieldtrip for their students,
