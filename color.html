Color Scheme Description

I wanted to use a professional color pallete, without getting too far away from a 'weather-ry' feeling. Due to this, I chose to use blue as my main color, while keeping black and white shades as main stays throughout the site. I also wanted to keep a beige and orange color for warmer weather, allowing a color to represent those weathers in the future.
