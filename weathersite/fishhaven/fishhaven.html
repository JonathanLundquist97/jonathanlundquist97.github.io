How Big Was That Fish Day

By: Garrett Stallar

April 1st Marks that day of the year again. The "How Big Was That Fish Day". That means Fish Haven gets together, and hosts our annual fishing contest, and gather around the fires and listen to our older citizens tell their fish based stories. One Fish Haven citizen remarked "I love how big was that fish day, not only do we get to listen to over inflated stories, and get to enjoy the whole day fishing, but also at the end of the day, the cook off is something that can't be missed. While only one can win, all the free dishes are winners in my book". Make sure you get down to the lake this April 1st and enjoy the holiday!
