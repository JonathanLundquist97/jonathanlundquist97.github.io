Geyser Song Day

By: Sammy Geysler

I love Geyser Song Day, all year round, we share our Geyer's with the world, often overlooking them as we carry about our lives around them. But every February 29th, we get together as a town and get to be in awe of nature's beauty that is living right next to us.

The Soda Spring Geyser was originally discovered in 1937 as the town was looking for hot water. Instead a rare naturally carbonated source of water was found, creating the geyser our town is now famous for. Heated by a natural source of lava, the geyser's water is usually around 70 degrees farenheit, and can reach heights of 100 feet into the air!
